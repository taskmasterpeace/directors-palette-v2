# Storyboard

**Route:** `/` (sidebar tab: Storyboard)
**Auth Required:** Yes

Transform scripts and stories into cinematic shot sequences with AI-generated visuals. Features:

- **AI Model Selector:** Choose the LLM for scene analysis (GPT-4.1 Mini default)
- **Mode Toggle:** Switch between "Storyboard" and "Documentary" modes
- **Story Text Editor:** Large text area for pasting scripts, screenplays, or story outlines. The AI automatically extracts characters and locations
- **Scene Analysis Engine:** AI breaks the story into individual shots with camera angles, lighting, and composition notes
- **Shot List:** Generated list of cinematic shots with chapter numbering (CX-NN format), title card injection, and up to 10 coherence suggestions
- **Batch Generation:** Generate all shot images at once using character references for consistency
- **10 Shot Types:** Wide, medium, close-up, extreme close-up, over-shoulder, POV, establishing, insert, two-shot, group
