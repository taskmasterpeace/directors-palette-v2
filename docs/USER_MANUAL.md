## 1. Getting Started <a name="getting-started"></a>
Navigate to the **Storyboard** tab to begin. You can paste your script or write a new story from scratch. The AI will analyze your text and break it down into cinematic shots.

## 2. The Storyboard <a name="the-storyboard"></a>
### Generating Shots
1.  Paste your script into the text area.
2.  Click **"Generate Shot Prompts"**.
3.  The system will create a list of shots, each with a proposed camera angle and description.

### Greenlight & Ratings
-   **Review:** Expand any shot to verify its details.
-   **Rate:** Use the **Star Rating** (0-5) to mark quality.
-   **Greenlight:** Toggle the **Greenlight** switch to approve a shot for production. Approved shots receive a green badge.

## 3. Director's Vision <a name="directors-vision"></a>
Transform your generic shots into stylized masterpieces by commissioning an AI Director.

### How to Commission
1.  In the Storyboard toolbar, click **"Commission Vision"** (Projector Icon).
2.  Select a Director from the list (e.g., **Ryan Cooler**, **Wes Sanderson**, **Hype Millions**).
3.  Click **"Apply Vision"**.
4.  The AI will rewrite all your prompts to match the director's signature style (lighting, composition, mood).

### Available Directors
-   **Ryan Cooler:** Dynamic camera movement, high contrast, emotional weight.
-   **Wes Sanderson:** Symmetrical composition, pastel palettes, whip pans.
-   **Hype Millions:** Fish-eye lenses, glossy futurism, vibrant colors.
-   **Clint Westwood:** Gritty realism, wide landscapes, stoic framing.
-   **David Pincher:** Low-key lighting, technical precision, psychological tension.

## 4. Shot Lab (Refinement) <a name="shot-lab"></a>
Use the **Shot Lab** to fine-tune specific shots using layout tools or AI masking.

### Accessing the Lab
Click the **"Refine Shot"** button (Flask Icon) on any generated shot card.

-   **Floating Toolbar:** Access tools easily with the glow-themed palette.

## 5. Prompt Syntax <a name="prompt-syntax"></a>
Advanced users can use special syntax in their prompts:

-   **Wildcards:** Use `[style]` or `[lighting]` to let the AI fill in details based on the active Director.
-   **Camera Angles:** `INT.` (Interior), `EXT.` (Exterior), `CU` (Close-up), `WS` (Wide Shot).
-   **Dynamic Values:** `{character}` placeholders will be replaced by defined character names.

## 6. Gallery & Organization <a name="gallery"></a>
Manage your generated assets in the **Gallery**.
-   **Folders:** Create folders to organize shots by scene or project. Colored badges help identify them.
-   **Filtering:** Filter by "Uncategorized" or specific folders.
-   **Bulk Actions:** Select multiple images to move or delete them in batches.
